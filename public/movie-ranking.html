Andrew's Top 3 Movies/TV Shows

Some critics believe that ranking movies and TV shows against each other is prohibited within the art of cinema. Well, Andrew just did by listing the titles, followed by his own scores.

Learn more about each film on IMDb by clicking on it's poster.

  • Breaking Bad 10/10

    • This TV show is regarded by many as the best of all time. It has the only 10/10 rated episode on IMDb for a reason. This masterpiece constantly presents eerily dark turns, leaving the audience in a state of constant suprise. The director, Vince Gilligan, did a fantastic job at creating addictive episodes, loaded with thrill and shock.


  • The Dark Knight 9.7/10

    • This film received a 9/10 on IMDb and 94% on Rotten Tomatoes. The role of the Joker, played by the late Heath Ledger, was the single greatest acting performance in cinematic history. Christian Bale's role as Batman was also presented with a spectacular acting performance.


  • Better Call Saul 9.6/10

    • Better Caul Saul is a prequel to Breaking Bad which features a small-time attorney, Saul Goodman, and his journey to becoming one of the most wanted criminals in America. The visual storytelling by the directors ensures the audience is kept on edge. This star-studded cast includes roles played by Bob Odenkirk, Rhea Seehorn, Jonathan Banks, Patrick Fabian, and more.
